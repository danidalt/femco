Our Leadership Team

Meet our fierce feminist team for the 2021 school year! FemCo is a nonhierarchical board composed of three leadership branches, Core Crew, Special Projects, and Ambassadors. We are newly launching our Ambassador program to encourage and grow relations with partnering organizations. Ambassadors will be responsible for attending monthly meetings to discuss current happenings, upcoming events, and collectively plan. Ambassadors along with respective organizations will be featured on our social media and website. If you think a leader in your community or you yourself would serve as a noble FemCo ambassador, please email Mary Champlin at hmchampl@ncsu.edu.

We are always so excited to develop and expand leadership oppertunties. In Fall 2020 we began serving on Council on the Status of Women, created Special Projects, and further secured relations with NC State's Women's Center. If your organization is interested in partnering or working with us, please email Ena Pojol at mspojol@ncsu.edu.
