About

The Feminist Collective of NC State “FemCo” is a club at North Carolina State University composed of driven leaders who strive to advance gender equity and social justice. We began our important work in the summer of 2019 and design our calendar each semester to reflect the needs and wants of our community. We work closely with NC State’s Campus Community Centers and collaborate with fifteen plus organizations located on and off-campus. Currently, our non-hierarchical leadership team consists of three branches, Core Crew, Special Projects, and Ambassadors. Check out our leadership page to learn more about our talented team.

Mission Statement

To elevate and support pre existing gender equity and social justice related clubs and organizations on NC State's campus that support, advocate for, and embody feminist principles.

Vision Statement

To hold space for gender equity and social justice clubs and organizations to connect, collaborate, and create in order to unify and mobilize around multidimentional feminist social justice efforts within our community and at large.

Our Values

FemCo's core values come from OXFAM Transformative and Feminist Leadership for Women’s Rights by Shawna Wakefeild. Click here to read more about OXFAM, and their important work to end global poverty.
