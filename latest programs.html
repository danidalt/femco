FemCo has several reoccurring monthly events, Feminist Forum, FemCo Full Moon Fiestas, General Body Meetings, and Tiny Talks. Each month new topics and ideas are explored to expand our feminist consciousnesses. In addition, we host a book club that follows the course of the semester. These graphics are just a little flavor of our last semester, Fall 2020, which was adapted to be fully online in light of the COVID-19 virus.

Past Programs

FemCo's events consist of a mixture of volunteering, collaborations with partnering organizations, socializers, attending marches, and educational seminars. Our schedule is organized differently each semester to reflect the needs and wants of the community. In lieu of Covid-19, all of our Fall 2020 events were virtual and volunteering opportunities were limited.
