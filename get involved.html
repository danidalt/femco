Get Connected, Get Involved!

There are many ways to get involved with FemCo! One of the most common methods of communication is Instagram. Follow us to hear our announcments and stay up to date on oppertunities. Don't have an Insta? No Worries. We also send out a weekly Newsletter, have a Facebook, and several group chats in order to communicate. If you fill out the "Sign up for our newsletter form" there is an option to join our GroupMe. Additionally, we have a Remind which sends out reminders for events and you can check us out on Get Involved to become an official member. Be sure to download our events calendar for SPRING 2020 calendar so you don't miss our rad events.

If you're interested in joining leadership, FemCo holds leadership elections towards the end of each semster. Check our our leadership page to learn more. Thanks!
